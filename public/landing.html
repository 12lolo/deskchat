How it works
  • Wallpaper fetches new messages every ~2.5s and shows ~30 recent ones.
  • Tray app sends posts to the API with a device ID (no account needed).
  • Server filters profanity, rate-limits abuse, and keeps the feed fast.
Privacy
  • No raw IPs stored by the app — only a hashed identifier server-side.
  • Messages auto-purge on a rolling schedule.
  • Anonymous by default: you choose an optional nickname.

FAQ

Do I need an account?

No — just set a nickname in the tray app and post.

Is it moderated?

Yes: server-side profanity filter and rate limiting. Abusive posts are blocked and posting too fast will temporarily rate-limit you.

Why can’t I see my post?

Ensure the tray app is running and you’re online. If you posted very quickly, you may be rate-limited for a short time.

GIFs & emojis?

Emojis work (Win + .). Tenor GIF links can be pasted; the wallpaper will render lightweight embeds.
